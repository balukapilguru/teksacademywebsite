About TEKS Academy Program

TEKS Academy is a part of Kapil Group located in Hyderabad, offering online and offline course training for students to become successful in their careers. Over the years, TEKS Academy launched several initiatives to shape a brighter future for students. To help every student access an excellent education, this year, TEKS Academy has started offering a scholarship program for various courses that help them build a dream career.

TEKS Academy has assisted 7000+ students so far in training them across various industries. Over 300+ highly qualified industry experts are present in our TEKS Academy, providing various knowledge and skills to our students. TEKS Academy provides in-demand skills like Salesforce, AWS + DevOps, Digital Marketing, Revit, Full stack Development, Data Science, and many certified courses, along with ISO, NASSCOM, Skill India, and NSDC recognition.
