Revit MEP

Download Syllabus

Revit MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) is a Building Information Modeling (BIM) software that allows engineers and designers to create and manage digital representations of building systems. The Revit MEP course covers the fundamentals of using Revit MEP software to design and analyze mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems in buildings.

Course Description

By precisely carrying out MEP Drafting Services and MEP BIM Services, Revit MEP enables MEP experts to produce MEP CAD Drafting and MEP Coordination Models. MEP, which stands for mechanical, electrical, and plumbing, is an acronym for three significant engineering fields.

The creation of sophisticated and intelligent models for HVAC (mechanical), electrical, and plumbing systems in any type of building is made possible by the widespread usage of Revit MEP software. The database contains a file with the smart model created by Revit MEP services, which accurately depicts the complete building project.

You will discover how to design HVAC, Hydronic, plumbing, fire protection, and electrical systems in this course. You will be introduced to Revit Interface at the beginning of the course. The fundamental abilities to choose elements, draw, and alter will then be covered. You will learn how to link Revit models from many disciplines in this course. You will learn how to design spaces before applying the new technique to do an energy analysis. You will learn both manual and automatic MEP system creation techniques in this course. Along with generating and printing sheets, you will learn how to set dimensions, write text, make tags, and build details for your design. Other advanced subjects, such as developing various HVAC schedules, will be covered throughout the course.

The creation of your own families of lighting fixtures, water heaters, and VAV boxes will be covered in the course. Finally, the course will show you how to import and link CAD data, link pictures, and PDF files, export your views to PDF files, and use other advanced Revit features.
