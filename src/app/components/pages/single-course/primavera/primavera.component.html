Course Description

Primavera P6 is a complete project management tool used to arrange project resources and activities for active portfolio management. You will master the various tools necessary to manage the project lifecycle effectively with the Primavera Training classes. You can proficiently complete a variety of project-related activities with the aid of these courses. You can learn how to organize and plan project activities, form enterprise task structures, and create organizational breakdown structures by taking one of these training courses. Primavera P6 tracking for various projects will be covered.

This course is focused on the industry and provides comprehensive explanations of all areas with detailed step-by-step instructions. This course will assist you in comprehending the significance and operation of Primavera P6 at all levels, from the most fundamental to the most sophisticated. Primavera P6 and project management advanced features are covered in the training. You will learn about projects, project management, their activities, their durations, linkages between activities, their types, lead and lag, and more. Primavera P6 will be introduced in the course, and students will leave knowing what an enterprise project structure is and how to add one.

In addition, it gives you an introduction to calendars and explains how to perform all of those things.
