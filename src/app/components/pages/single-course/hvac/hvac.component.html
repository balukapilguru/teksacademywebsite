HVAC

Download Syllabus

Heating, ventilation, and air conditioning are usually referred to as HVAC. This is the system in the structure that deals with the interior temperature and, in certain systems, the air quality. The fundamental principles of system operation and the elements that influence the size and capacity of the installed equipment are involved in the principles of HVAC design. You will be given knowledge about the many kinds of air conditioning systems once you have a strong grasp of the basics.

Course Description

The following stage is to give you knowledge about the many parts that contain an HVAC system. The many refrigerants that can be found in air conditioning systems are fully covered because, for example, the air conditioning element of an HVAC system needs refrigerant to chill the air within the building. Finally, you’ll learn about the particulars of installing an HVAC system, such as where you should put the compressor and condenser units, as well as where to place the zones, controls, and heat exchangers.

HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) Training Courses cover the essentials of HVAC Systems and Mechanisms, system design, HVAC drawings and drafting, equipment erection, project estimation, equipment maintenance, and detail engineering of Central HVAC Systems, including DX Air Systems and Chilled Water Systems, with special training sessions focusing on the specific applications and usage of codes and standards. Experts offer in-depth education, practice, and helpful exercises throughout the course.

The training provides professionals of all skill levels, from beginners to specialists, with Designing and Drafting skills. By investigating the tools and methods for dimensioning, printing, and sketching, the learning curve is broadened. The candidate must be able to understand thermodynamics and heat transport theories as they relate to the commercial HVAC industry at the end of the course.

You will learn how to make a cooling load calculation and select the appropriate type of air conditioning system for a project in this course. Your understanding of designing chilled water systems, air distribution systems, and engineering calculations like ESP calculations and pump head calculations will be great. Additionally, you’ll learn how to pick the appropriate tools for the job.
