Course Description:

PGDCA (Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Application) is designed to give students a clear understanding about computer applications. The course mainly focuses on the practical and theoretical knowledge of computers and their applications. The course provides knowledge about operating systems, software engineering and database management systems.

The course provides the necessary skills and knowledge to become more skillful in computer applications. This course helps students get into jobs in various industries.

Why is PGDCA important?

PGDCA is one of the professionals programmed that can help you acquire a deeper understanding of computer applications. PGDCA is also designed for graduate students who pursue specialization and have sufficient computer-related technical knowledge.

The diploma programmed enables you to enter the professional world and obtain a higher-paying position in the industry. You will have flexibility in learning the entire course, which is one of the course’s primary advantages.

What does PGDCA provide you with?

The PGDCA covers every basic to intermediate computer application that is essential in the IT industry. Our PGDCA provides the following takeaways.

  • You will excel at Microsoft Office applications.
  • In-depth detail about the software and hardware.
  • Entire working of the programming and the computer system.
  • Shortcuts that are used in everyday computer applications.

Who should take the PGDCA course?

Unlike other courses PGDCA does not take more time to complete. You can master the basic computer application and shortcuts in a very short time.

  • Students who earned a bachelor’s degree.
  • Working professionals who require a computer skills upgrade.
  • Students interested in pursuing a career in computer science.
