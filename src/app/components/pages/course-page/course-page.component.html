About

Develop Your Skills, Learn Something New, and Grow Your Career!

TEKS Academy is one of the best Data Science Training Institutes in Hyderabad, distinguished by its commitment to excellence. Boasting experts with 10 years of experience, TEKS Academy provides an advanced curriculum that covers the latest advancements in data science, ensuring students are well prepared for the interviews. The academy stands out by providing a flexible learning environment with both offline and online programs. Knowing about the financial conditions of education, TEKS Academy offers a no-cost EMI option for fees, making quality education accessible to all. Moreover, the academy's dedication extends beyond education, guaranteeing 100% placement assistance to students. In essence, TEKS Academy not only provides knowledge but also serves as a career catalyst, preparing students for success in the competitive world of data science.

The course curriculum covers Fundamentals of Statistics and Mathematics, learning of data science programming languages, exploratory data analysis techniques, and learning of visualisation tools like Matplotlib, Plotly, and Seaborn. Our Data Science placement assistance team helps students and learners develop their skills to their excellence and land their dream jobs. Our perfectly crafted Data Science training program helps you learn about data visualisation, predictive modelling, feature engineering, big data technologies, natural language processing, deep learning, and many other topics.
